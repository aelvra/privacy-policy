Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 20, 2025

I, Aelvra, developed the ArixQ Calc app as a free application. This app is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is. Your privacy is critically important to me.

1. Information Collection

ArixQ Calc does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

  • No analytics or tracking (No Firebase Analytics, No Facebook SDK).
  • No user accounts or login required.
  • No third-party advertising.

2. Local Data Storage

All data generated by the App remains exclusively on your device:

  • Calculation History: Stored locally using sqflite. You can clear this at any time.
  • App Preferences: Settings like Theme, Vibration, and Converter Categories are saved using shared_preferences.

I (the Developer) have no access to this data.

3. Third-Party Services & Technical Data (GDPR Notice)

The App uses specific third-party services to function. While the App itself does not collect personal data, connecting to these services requires standard internet communication.

Frankfurter.app (Currency Rates):
To provide currency conversion, the App fetches rates from Frankfurter.app. When your device requests data, your IP address is technically visible to the API provider to fulfill the request. This is a standard function of the internet. We do not log this data.

Google Fonts:
The App may load font files from Google’s servers.

4. Permissions Declaration

  • android.permission.INTERNET: Used strictly to fetch currency rates from the API.
  • android.permission.VIBRATE: Used for haptic feedback on button presses.

5. Open Source Libraries Used

The App utilizes the following open-source libraries, which operate locally and do not collect user data:

  • sqflite & path_provider (Storage)
  • shared_preferences (Settings)
  • math_expressions & function_tree (Calculation)
  • http (API Requests)

6. Children’s Privacy

ArixQ Calc does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates are effective immediately when posted on this page.
