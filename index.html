Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 18, 2025

I, Aelvra, developed the ArixQ Calc app as a free application. This app is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how the ArixQ Calc app handles information.

1. Information I Collect (And Don’t Collect)

  • ArixQ Calc does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • No analytics, telemetry, or tracking are used.
  • No calculations, history, or preferences are collected.
  • No third-party advertising or trackers are used.
  • All data remains on your device.

2. Local Data Handling

All app data is stored locally on your device.

Calculation History

  • Stored locally using sqflite.
  • You may delete it anytime.
  • I cannot access it.

App Preferences

Saved using shared_preferences:

  • Theme
  • Vibration
  • Converter category order
  • Cached currency rates

None of this data leaves your device.

3. Permissions Used

  • android.permission.INTERNET
    Used only to fetch currency exchange rates from Frankfurter.app.
  • android.permission.VIBRATE
    Used for haptic feedback.

No personal data is transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

Frankfurter.app (Currency Rates)

  • Used to fetch current exchange rates.
  • Requests are anonymous.
  • No personal information is shared.

Google Fonts

May load font files from Google’s servers.

Other Open-Source Libraries

All operate locally:

  • sqflite
  • path_provider
  • shared_preferences
  • http
  • url_launcher
  • vibration
  • function_tree
  • math_expressions
  • reorderable_grid_view

None of these collect any data.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored only on your device’s private storage.
I do not upload or access this data.
Please download the app only from the official Google Play Store.

6. Children’s Privacy

ArixQ Calc does not collect any personal information and is safe for children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.
Updates are effective immediately when posted.
